Peak Performance Taekwondo Academy was established in 2013 by Jibreel Malik.



We aim to inspire people to improve their fitness, health as well as learning how to defend themselves and take part in competitive sport through the Olympic Martial art of Taekwondo.



We believe that every individual regardless of age, gender or race is capable of achieving greatness in any aspect of life.



We are a friendly, energetic and a motivated club. We compete up and down the country in local, national and international competitions and encourage people to join Peak Performance Taekwondo Academy even if you have no interest in competition.



Our Coaches are highly qualified and experienced instructors who have all had experience at international level competition representing Great Britain. This is to ensure our classes are managed properly. Peak Performance Academy instructors are CRB/DBS checked and will provide students with high quality martial arts instruction and a safe environment for any age or sex to train in.
